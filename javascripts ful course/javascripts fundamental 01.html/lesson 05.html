basic operators

operators are used to perform specific mathematical and logical computation on operands, can be used to compare values, perform arithmetic operations. they can be used to transform and combine multiple values.

types of operators

  • arithmetic / mathematical operator
  • comparison operator
  • assignment operator
  • logical operator
arithmetic operator
arithemetic operators perform arithmetic on numbers

types of arithemetic operators

  • substraction
  • addition
  • multiplication
  • division
  • modulo known as percent
comparison operator
comparison operators are used in logical to determine equality between or difference between variables or values

types of comparison operators

  • = = means equal to
  • != means not equal to
  • = = = means strictly equal to
  • != = means strictly not equal to
  • >= greater than or equal to
  • <= less than or equal to
  • > greater than
  • < less than
assignment operators
assignment operators assigns a value to its left operand based on the value of its right operand. the simple assignment operator is = sign
logical operators
logical operators are used to connect two or more expressions.

types of logical operators

  • AND &&
  • NOT !
  • OR ||
